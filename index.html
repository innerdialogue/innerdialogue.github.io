About Inner Dialogue

At Inner Dialogue, each consellor believes that every living being is capable of becoming self-realized, and one who realizes this is bound to become a realized soul. Therapists here strive to become a part of client's this journey towards awareness to lead fulfilling life. They are determined to spread awareness about mental health in society by working hard and having strong ethics.

About Team

Our team of counsellors here comes from diverse backgrounds – some have prior experience in fields as different as HR, IT, Coaching and Social Work, but common to this team is their professional competence in counselling and psychology, and passion to make a difference. All counsellors here have minimum of a Masters in counselling psychology, clinical psychology. Many are having Ph.D’s and several years of counselling experience. Our therapists here invest substantial amount of effort in providing a flexible combination of mode of therapy, location of therapy and language of therapy suitable to our clients with affordable fees.
